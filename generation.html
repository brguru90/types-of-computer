Types of computer based on generation:-

First Generation(1940-56):Vaccum Tubes:

The first generation computer were developed in the year 1945 by the U.S>Army research unit.The computer was called as ENIAC(Electronic Numeric Integrator And calculator) used to calculate the trajectories of the missiles.ENIAC took about 200 milisecond to add 2 digit and 280 microsecond to multiply.John von Neumann, a professor used the concept of stored program and designed a machine called as EDSAC.(Electronic Delay Storage Automatic computer).The first commercial production of stored Electroonic computer was UNIVAC(Universal Automic Computer).
eg.:ENIAC,UNIVAC-I

Fetures

  1. The vaccum tubes took long time to get heated up and the processing speed of such machines was slow.
  2. These machines were very large in size and occupied more space.
  3. The component in these nachine generated lot of heat and thus needed more maintenece for longer life.
  4. System cost and woring cost is very high.
  5. First generation computer were built on the concept of stored program.
  6. Programs and data were fed through punched paper tape.
